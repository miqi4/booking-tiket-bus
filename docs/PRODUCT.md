# Product Context: Bus Akas (Reliable Transit)

## Purpose
A web-based bus ticket booking system for Bus Akas, focusing on the Indonesian transport sector. The goal is to provide a reliable, fast, and professional booking experience.

## Users
- **Passengers**: Indonesian travelers looking for reliable bus transportation. They value clarity, speed, and trust.
- **Operators/Admins**: Staff managing schedules, bookings, and passengers.

## Brand Personality
- **Professional & Authoritative**: Signals stability and established service.
- **Accessible**: Easy to use, reducing anxiety associated with travel booking.
- **Modern Corporate**: High-quality typography and ample whitespace.

## Register
product

## Strategic Principles
- **Reliability First**: Every interaction should build trust.
- **Logistical Precision**: Accurate data display for schedules and seat maps.
- **Accessibility**: Clear labels and semantic HTML for diverse user groups.
